About Revenza

1. Our Mission

Our Mission at Revenza

At Revenza, we build intelligent solutions that help merchants unlock higher conversions and maximize revenue, without compromising the customer experience.

Our flagship product, Revenza Upsell Addons, enables store owners to present highly relevant add-on products at the right moment, enhancing average order value while maintaining a seamless and elegant shopping journey.

We believe that effective upselling should feel natural, not intrusive. That is why our approach focuses on precision, performance, and a refined user experience, benefiting both merchants and their customers.

Revenza - designed to help you sell smarter, not harder.

2. Revenza Story

How It Started

Every good product has a story. Ours started with two colleagues and one brilliant idea.

We are Sarthak Jha (Developer) and Yatish Vijay (Support Expert), two friends who met at work, bonded over challenges, and realized we shared the same drive: to build something meaningful for merchants.

The turning point came from a simple suggestion during a casual evening conversation: "Why don't you guys build something together?"

That idea became a mission. We combined technical expertise with deep customer understanding and built products focused on helping Shopify merchants increase revenue, improve conversions, and deliver a better shopping experience.
