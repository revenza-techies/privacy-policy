Our Story

Every good product has a story. Ours started with two colleagues and one brilliant idea.

We are Sarthak Jha (Developer) and Yatish Vijay (Support Expert), two friends who met at work, bonded over challenges, and eventually realized we shared something bigger: the drive to build something meaningful.

Like many builders, we both tried our own paths, faced our own struggles, and learned things the hard way. But the turning point did not come from a whiteboard session or a business meeting.

It came from Sarthak's wife.

One evening, during a casual conversation (with a few drinks involved), she dropped a simple idea:

"Why don't you guys build something together?"

That was it. No complicated planning. No overthinking.

Just two friends, one idea, and the decision to start that very night.

What Happened Next

What began as a spontaneous decision quickly turned into a mission.

We combined:

  • Technical expertise (Sarthak)
  • Customer understanding and operations (Yatish)

And built our first product focused on helping Shopify merchants:

  • Increase revenue
  • Improve conversions
  • Deliver a better shopping experience

Where We're Headed

Revenza is not just one project, it is the beginning.

We are committed to continuously building solutions that help merchants:

  • Sell smarter
  • Grow faster
  • Deliver a seamless, high-quality experience to their customers

Because in the end, great businesses are not just about selling more, they are about creating experiences customers actually enjoy.

In Simple Words

Two friends. One unexpected idea. And a journey that started over a drink, and is now focused on helping merchants grow better every day.

And we are just getting started.
