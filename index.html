This Privacy Policy describes how Revenza Techies collects, uses, and protects information when you install or use the Revenza Upsell Addon App on your Shopify store.

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Revenza Techies ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you install or use the Revenza Upsell Addon App ("the App") on your Shopify store.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you install and use our App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Store Information: Shopify store domain, store email, and basic shop details.
  • Product Data: Product IDs, titles, variants, and related metadata required to display upsell recommendations.
  • App Configuration Data: Settings and product selections configured within the App.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage logs for performance and debugging purposes.

We do not collect or store sensitive personal customer data unless explicitly required for app functionality.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to provide and improve the App’s functionality, including:

  • Displaying upsell and recommended products on your store
  • Saving and managing your app settings and configurations
  • Authenticating and identifying your Shopify store
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Providing customer support when requested

We do not sell, rent, or misuse your data.

4. Shopify API Usage

Our App accesses Shopify store data via Shopify APIs only as necessary to provide core functionality.

We access limited store data such as product information to generate and display upsell recommendations.

We do not access or process customer personal data unless explicitly required and permitted by Shopify and the merchant.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

We may share data only with trusted service providers, such as hosting or infrastructure providers, strictly required to operate the App. These providers are obligated to protect your data.

We may also disclose data if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on cloud infrastructure and protected using reasonable administrative and technical safeguards.

While we take appropriate measures to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the App’s services and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Upon uninstallation of the App, we automatically delete or anonymize your store-related data from our database within a reasonable timeframe, unless we are required to retain certain information for legal, compliance, or legitimate business purposes.

If you would like to request deletion of your data at any time, you may contact us using the details provided below.

8. Your Rights

As a merchant, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we store about your store
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Stop data processing by uninstalling the App

For any requests, you can contact us using the details below.
